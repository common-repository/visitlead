== Installation ==

Install:

* Choose 'Plugins > Add New' in Wordpress and search 'visitlead'


Activate:

1. Go to the 'Plugins' menu in Wordpress and click on 'Activate' in the VISITLEAD row
2. Click on 'Settings' and enter your Visitlead ID
3. Live Chat should be available after reloading your page

Get the CID from your <a href="https://VISITLEAD.com/login" target="_blank" style="margin: 0 3px">existing Visitlead account</a> - OR - <a href="https://VISITLEAD.com/register" target="_blank">create a new Visitlead account </a>.
